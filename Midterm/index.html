About Us

Solarview Design & Installation offers custom window treatments including aluminum, wood, faux wood, and blinds. Along with interior and exterior roller shades and shutters - all designed to fit the needs of our discerning clients.

We service projects of all sizes from single residential to multi-family and commercial jobs. Our experienced team can meet just about any architectural specification. We are the experts on all of your motorization and home automation needs for window treatments.

From design to installation, we take pride in offering quality products as well as worry-free professional installation and workmanship.

Servicing the Greater St. Louis, MO/ Southern Illinois Market.

Located in Edwardsville, IL.
